üìñ Blockchain Glossary

Blockchain

A distributed digital ledger that records transactions across multiple computers in a way that makes them difficult to change or hack.

Cryptocurrency

A digital or virtual currency secured by cryptography, making it nearly impossible to counterfeit or double-spend.

Smart Contract

Self-executing contracts with terms directly written into code, automatically enforcing agreements without intermediaries.

Decentralization

The distribution of functions and powers away from a central authority to a network of participants.
